Egg Salad Recipe

Egg salad is the best lunch for a healthy diet. Save some money and make your own at home!

Ingredients:
  • 12 hard-cooked eggs, divided
  • ¼ cup low-fat mayonnaise
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh chives
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ½ teaspoon celery salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground paprika
  • ½ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
Steps:
  1. Peel 8 eggs. Chop eggs (both whites and yolks) and transfer to a medium bowl.
  2. Peel remaining 4 eggs; separate whites and yolks; reserve yolks for another use. Chop the whites and add to the bowl.
  3. Add mayonnaise, chives, Dijon, celery salt, paprika, kosher salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined.
