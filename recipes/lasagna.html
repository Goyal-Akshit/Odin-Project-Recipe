Lasagna Recipe

Lasagna is the only food able to please any kind of crowd. Made with thick meaty sauce, enveloping the noodles and filled with surprise joys of cheese!! Once baked can be frozen and enjoyed for days.

Ingredients
  • Virgin Olive Oil
  • Meat of choice
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese of choice
  • Salt and spices
  1. Brown meat of choice with a little olive oil.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes and spices.
  3. Simmer till fragrant.
  4. Add salt to taste.
  5. Layer sauce, noodles and cheese in a deepdish.
  6. Bake tille bubbly!
